# Treat Yourself: Sweet Treats & Boba Guide around NYU 💜

*Written with love by someone who really, really loves cookies and boba*

Midterm season is here, and finals are just around the corner... hope everyone is doing okay. In between your study sessions, if you are looking to treat yourself, I got you.

Here is my little guide to some of my favorite sweet treats and boba spots around NYU (and nearby), so you can give yourself a little more fuel as you prepare for your exams!

## 🧋 BOBA + DRINKS

- **[Debutea](https://www.debutea.com/)**
  - Locations:
    - Manhattan: 217 Thompson St, New York, NY 10012.
    - Brooklyn: 71 Smith St, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
  - Go-to orders: Coco Mango (a must!), Soy Milk Tea, Milky Strawberry, Milky Papaya (favorite seasonal special).
  - Brooklyn location notes: This location has seating, a bar area, Wi-Fi, and charging outlets. It’s very study-friendly, and many students come here to do work or study for exams.
- **[Hey Tea](https://heyteaus.org/)**
  - Top recs: Cloud Mango, Coconut Mango Boom
  - Milk tea faves: Matcha Jasmine Latte, Supreme Brown Sugar Boba Milk Tea
  - Locations:
    - 10 Waverly Pl (right by NYU!).
    - 2nd Ave in East Village (new!).
    - 275 Bleecker St in Manhattan West Village
- **[Luckin Coffee](https://www.luckincoffee.us/)**
  - Locations:
    - 755 Broadway, New York, NY 10003(nearest).
  - A super convenient grab-and-go coffee spot that’s perfect for busy study days.
  - Go-to drinks: Iced Coconut Latte, Classic Latte, Americano, and seasonal specialty drinks (they rotate a lot, so always check the app!).
  - Very budget-friendly compared to most NYC coffee shops, especially with frequent app discounts.
- **[Mixue](https://mixuemenu.us/new-york-city/)**
  - Locations:
    - Manhattan: 266 Canal St, New York, NY 10013.
    - Brooklyn: 519 Fulton St, Brooklyn, NY 11201(new!).
  - Go-to picks: Lemon Water with 200% sugar, and Ice Cream (a must for a quick treat)!
  - Very budget-friendly and tastes great!
  - Also a solid late-night option — open pretty late (usually around 10pm, some locations even until 12am), perfect for post-study cravings 🌙.
- **Lazy Sundaes**
  - Personal fave: Strawberry Matcha 🍓🍵
  - Cute vibe and aesthetic!
- **Chihiro**
  - 10% Student Discount
  - Faves: Thai Tea, Mango Pomelo Sago
  - Refreshing and not overly sweet.
- **Jooy Tea (8th St NYU)**
  - Faves: Matcha Tornado, Jasmine Milk Tea, Crème Brûlée Milk Tea
  - Great balance of tea flavor and sweetness.

## 🍪 COOKIES + PASTRIES

- **Levain Bakery**
  - Must-trys: Chocolate Chip Walnut and Dark Chocolate Chocolate Chip
  - These are thick and gooey cookies!
- **Lafayette Bakery**
  - Famous for their Croissant Suprême – get the Pistachio flavor if you can!
- **LA LA Bakeshop (2nd Ave)**
  - Vietnamese bakery with AMAZING Salted Egg Lava Bun, Pandan Tiramisu, and Tiramisu Latte
- **Donut Pub**
  - The classic donut spot 🍩. Can’t go wrong – just get one, or five.
- **Hanis Bakery**
  - Personal obsessions: Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie, Cinnamon Roll
- **[Martha’s Bakery](https://marthascountrybakery.com/)**
  - Location:
    - 175 Bedford Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11211.
  - A cozy neighborhood bakery with a wide variety of cakes, pastries, and Middle Eastern-inspired sweets.
  - Go-to order: Berry Napoleon 🍓 (honestly the best thing I’ve ever eaten here — if you try it and agree, report it as “too good”)
  - Prices are pretty budget-friendly for NYC.
  - A solid go-to when you want something more “classic bakery” style compared to cookie-heavy spots like Levain.

Exam season can be stressful, but that doesn't mean you can’t take care of yourself. Treats don’t solve everything, but sometimes, sweet desserts and a moment to rest and breathe can help you feel better.

So go ahead, take that boba break. Grab that dessert. You deserve it!
